Welcome to the Manama Rural District Chamber of Commerce

Breaking News: Local Farmers Shine in SADC Competitions!

We are thrilled to announce that two of our exceptional farmers, Goodwill and Reaboka, have brought great pride to our rural district in Africa. They recently participated in the prestigious SADC (Southern African Development Community) competitions and achieved remarkable success. Goodwill secured a gold medal, while Reaboka earned a silver medal, showcasing their expertise and dedication to agriculture. Their outstanding achievements highlight the immense talent within our farming community.

Exciting Netball Match at Manama Stadium Sponsored by Goodwill Pharmacies!

Mark your calendars for a thrilling netball match between the teams of Mkhalipe and Seboza, set to take place at the renowned Manama Stadium. We are delighted to announce that this exciting sporting event is proudly sponsored by Goodwill Pharmacies, a trusted and community-oriented establishment. Join us as we cheer for our talented athletes, support teamwork, and celebrate the spirit of sportsmanship in our vibrant district.
